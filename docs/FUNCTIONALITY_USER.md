# Sankeertanotsav 2026 Live - Platform Overview

## Welcome to Sankeertanotsav Live

Sankeertanotsav Live is a comprehensive digital platform designed to bring the magic of Indian classical music concerts directly to audiences worldwide. Whether you're in Chennai, California, or Copenhagen, you can experience premium performances from the comfort of your home.

---

## What We've Built

### A Complete Concert Experience Online

This platform has been carefully crafted to replicate the experience of attending a live concert while adding the convenience of digital access. Every aspect—from discovering events to watching performances to accessing recordings—has been thoughtfully designed.

---

## Platform Capabilities

### 1. Discover Concerts

**Browse Without Barriers**
- View all upcoming and past concerts without needing to create an account
- See concert details including artist information, date, time, and descriptions
- Separate sections for premium (paid) concerts and free community events

**Smart Time Display**
- Concert times are shown in India Standard Time (IST) for consistency
- If you're in a different timezone, your local time is also displayed automatically
- No confusion about when to tune in, regardless of where you are in the world

---

### 2. Flexible Ticketing Options

**Individual Concert Tickets**
- Purchase access to specific concerts that interest you
- One-time payment grants access to both the live stream and the recording
- Secure payment processing through Razorpay

**Season Ticket (Best Value)**
- A single purchase that covers all premium concerts
- Access to every paid event from your purchase date onwards
- Includes both live streaming and recording access for all covered concerts
- Significant savings compared to buying individual tickets

**Seamless Purchase Flow**
- Start browsing as a guest, decide to buy, and create your account in one smooth flow
- Your intended purchase is remembered even if you need to register first
- No lost carts or forgotten selections

---

### 3. Live Streaming Experience

**Professional-Grade Streaming**
- Powered by Amazon Web Services Interactive Video Service (AWS IVS)
- Ultra-low latency means you see the performance almost in real-time
- Adaptive quality adjusts to your internet connection automatically
- Reliable delivery even during peak viewership

**Live Indicators**
- Clear "LIVE" badges show when a concert is currently streaming
- Real-time viewer counts let you feel part of the community
- Automatic detection of stream status

**Free Events via YouTube**
- Community and promotional events streamed via YouTube
- No account or payment required
- Same integrated viewing experience within the platform

---

### 4. Recording Access

**Never Miss a Moment**
- All paid concerts are automatically recorded
- Recordings available for 3 days after the concert ends
- Watch at your convenience within the access window

**Clear Expiry Information**
- Countdown shows exactly how much time remains to watch
- "Recording expires: 2d 5h left" style notifications
- Plan your viewing accordingly

**Multi-Part Recordings**
- Concerts with multiple sessions (e.g., morning and evening ragas) are organized clearly
- Easy navigation between different parts of a performance

## Behind the Scenes: Infrastructure

### Domain & Web Presence

**Custom Domain Registration**
- Professional web address for the platform
- Memorable URL that represents the Sankeertanotsav brand

**DNS Configuration**
- Domain properly pointed to hosting servers
- Secure HTTPS encryption for all connections
- Fast loading through optimized routing

---

### Cloud Infrastructure

**Production Environment**
- Hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) for reliability
- Servers located in Mumbai region for optimal performance in India
- Automatic scaling to handle audience surges during popular concerts
- 24/7 availability with redundancy built in

**Database Systems**
- Secure storage of user accounts and ticket information
- Payment records maintained with full audit trails
- Event data organized for quick retrieval

---

### Live Streaming Infrastructure

**AWS Interactive Video Service (IVS)**
- Enterprise-grade live streaming service
- Same technology used by Twitch and major broadcasters
- Sub-second latency for near-real-time viewing
- Automatic quality adaptation based on viewer's connection

**Channel Configuration**
- Dedicated streaming channels for each concert
- Secure ingest endpoints for broadcaster equipment
- Multiple quality renditions generated automatically

---

### Automated Recording System

**AWS S3 Storage Integration**
- All live streams automatically recorded to cloud storage
- No manual intervention required—recordings happen seamlessly
- Secure, encrypted storage with controlled access

**Recording Workflow**
- Stream starts → Recording begins automatically
- Stream ends → Recording finalized and made available
- 3-day access window → Recording archived/removed

**Storage Organization**
- Recordings organized by event and date
- Multiple sessions within a concert tracked separately
- Efficient retrieval for playback

---

### Payment Processing

**Razorpay Integration**
- India's leading payment gateway
- Supports credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets
- Secure transaction processing with encryption
- Instant payment confirmation

**Order Management**
- Unique order IDs for every transaction
- Payment verification before granting access
- Complete transaction history maintained

---

### Security Measures

**User Data Protection**
- Passwords encrypted using industry-standard methods
- Personal information stored securely
- No payment card details stored on our servers

**Access Control**
- Ticket verification before allowing stream access
- Session management to prevent unauthorized sharing
- Admin-only areas protected with role-based access

## The User Journey

### For a First-Time Visitor

1. **Discover**: Land on the platform and browse upcoming concerts
2. **Explore**: Click on a concert to see full details
3. **Decide**: Choose to buy an individual ticket or season pass
4. **Register**: Create an account (takes under a minute)
5. **Pay**: Complete secure payment via Razorpay
6. **Watch**: Return when the concert goes live and enjoy!

### For a Returning User

1. **Login**: Quick access with email and password
2. **Browse**: See what's new and what you have access to
3. **Watch**: One click to join a live stream or view a recording

### For Season Ticket Holders

1. **Login**: Your subscription is automatically recognized
2. **Access All**: Every paid concert is available to you
3. **Enjoy**: No additional purchases needed for covered events
